# 咪蒙写作蒸馏

## Overview

Use this skill to convert a topic into an original high-emotion Chinese WeChat-style opinion piece using reusable mechanisms extracted from the corpus: conflict-first angle selection, second-person reader pressure, short paragraph rhythm, anecdote stacking, reversal, and a value-judgment close.

This skill is not a style-cloning skill. Never claim to write as 咪蒙, never reuse source titles or sentences, and never preserve a distinctive passage merely by swapping words.

## Quick Workflow

1. Define the brief in one line: `topic + target reader + core conflict + desired position + intensity`.
2. Select the article lane: relationship, money/class, workplace/growth, family, public event, reader story, or social judgment.
3. Read [references/title-and-angle.md](references/title-and-angle.md) when generating angles, titles, or openings.
4. Read [references/article-architecture.md](references/article-architecture.md) when drafting a full article or restructuring a weak draft.
5. Read [references/voice-and-rhythm.md](references/voice-and-rhythm.md) when calibrating paragraph rhythm, direct address, humor, reversal, and emotional intensity.
6. Always run [references/originality-and-safety.md](references/originality-and-safety.md) before finalizing.

## Default Deliverables

When the user asks for a full article, provide:

- 5 to 10 original title options using different molds.
- A recommended title with the emotional promise named.
- The article draft, usually 2200 to 3500 Chinese characters unless the user specifies another length.
- Optional: a short note naming the chosen structure, intensity level, and any factual assumptions.

When the user asks for a lighter task, provide only what they requested: title options, opening rewrites, outline, angle list, or diagnosis.

## Working Rules

- Start from a reader wound, not an abstract topic.
- Turn the topic into a conflict between two values: dignity vs compromise, love vs control, money vs face, family duty vs selfhood, effort vs unfairness.
- Prefer concrete social scenes over generic preaching.
- Use strong judgment only after evidence, anecdote, or relatable tension has been established.
- Keep paragraphs short. One sentence can be a paragraph when it carries a turn, punchline, or verdict.
- Use numbers, roles, money amounts, and everyday objects as anchors, but do not fabricate factual claims.
- Avoid overusing profanity or insults. Replace cruelty with a sharp, defensible position.

## Refusal And Redirect

If the user asks to copy the author, reproduce passages, rewrite a specific source article too closely, or “写得像咪蒙本人”, respond by offering an original high-emotion WeChat article using the distilled mechanisms. Do not continue with author impersonation or close imitation.

## Corpus Notes

This skill was distilled from a local PDF corpus containing 6700 pages and roughly 624 usable article segments. Only aggregate patterns, reusable structures, and safety constraints are stored here. Source text is not bundled.
